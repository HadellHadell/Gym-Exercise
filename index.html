Welcome to our gym!

We are a professional organization with new and fresh gym equipment. In the gym you will find our well educated staff which will be there to help and motivate you throughout your training session. The gym is for everyone who is interested in what it has to offer. no matter if you are a beginner or an athlete, young or old you are welcome. Our organization has something that fits for everybody and if you don't believe us please contact us and come and try the gym out. You will find our contact information under the ‘Contact’ page.

When you are done with your training session you are more than welcome to use our relaxing area equiped with comfy furniture and wending machines. In the locker rooms you will also find separete showers for privacy and a sauna for deep relaxsation.
